## 📌 Overview

**SugandhSetu** is a premium agarbatti and dhoop booking ecosystem designed to bring the timeless tradition of Indian fragrances into the digital age. With a rich, heritage-inspired UI, the platform offers a seamless experience for customers to explore, discover, and order authentic incense products from trusted brands.

> 🌿 *"Handcrafted fragrances, temple-grade incense, and curated wellness collections — delivered to your doorstep with love."*

---

## ✨ Features

| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **🪔 Product Catalog** | Browse 50+ premium agarbatti, dhoop, temple packs, gift sets, and wellness products. |
| **🏷️ Categories** | Explore curated collections — Agarbatti, Dhoop, Temple Packs, Gift Sets, Festival Special, Wellness. |
| **🌟 Best Sellers** | Handpicked products with ratings, prices, and quick add-to-cart functionality. |
| **🎉 Festival Banner** | Special Diwali Mega Pack promotion with a call-to-action for seasonal offers. |
| **⭐ Testimonials** | Real customer stories with star ratings and authentic feedback. |
| **📊 Live Stats** | Animated counters showing products, brands, orders delivered, and customer ratings. |
| **📧 Newsletter** | Subscribe to receive exclusive offers, new arrivals, and festival updates. |
| **💎 Premium UI** | Glassmorphism, gold accents, burgundy tones, and a luxurious heritage aesthetic. |
| **🖱️ Custom Cursor** | Interactive gold-glowing cursor for an immersive browsing experience. |
| **📱 Fully Responsive** | Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. |

## 🎨 Design Theme

- **Color Palette:** Gold (`#C9A84C`), Burgundy (`#6B1A2A`), Warm Brown (`#4A2C1A`), Cream (`#FDF8F0`), Dark Background (`#1A0F0A`)
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (serif) for headings — Inter (sans-serif) for body
- **Visual Style:** Glassmorphism, soft shadows, glowing gold accents, organic curved layouts, and floating incense smoke animations.
- **Loading Screen:** Incense stick with glowing ember and rising smoke animation — a unique, brand-aligned experience.
- **Animations:** Gold shimmer, floating particles, smoke rise, hover transitions, and subtle sparkle effects.
